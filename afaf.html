Anything For A Friend

This was an N-tier project using ASP.NET to build an event registration service that would allow users to sign up through AFAF's website. This would allow them to take the profits which they would normally pay event hosting sites and give it all to the families in need.

The class was split into 3 tiers (Presentation, Business Logic, and Database) and I was placed in the Presentation tier as the lead designer. I designed all the mockups for the site's layout and look while getting feedback from my team and Becky. I also worked with the others on the html/css and validation.

The project was completed on the presentation side by the end of the course, and mostly completed by the other tiers. We were able to give a presentation to Becky and her team, and they were very thrilled about it.

The full checkout cycle was implemented but more time was still needed to have the service ready for the next fundraiser, and the next semester students would be finishing our project and adding some extra features.
