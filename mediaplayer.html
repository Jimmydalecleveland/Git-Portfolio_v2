Office Media Player

The first phase of this UML course consisted of splitting up into groups of 4-5 and going through a series of requirement-gathering meetings. We were then told to each come up with a user interface based on the specifications and the best version would be selected as the UI for our group. My first draft was selected as our group's UI, although it was a close choice between another group member's.

At the end of the course all the groups presented their UI's and some documentation. I was very proud that my UI (after several revisions since the first draft) won the vast majority of votes for the best in the class.
